Come
join the pack.

Four short steps to get your Scout in. We'll walk you through it — and if you'd rather just show up to a meeting first and see what it's about, do that instead.

How it works

Four steps. About fifteen minutes.

01

Drop us a note

Fill out the short form below. A real pack parent replies within two business days — no robots, no sales pitch.

02

Visit a meeting

Come to a Pack Meeting. Bring your Scout. Watch a night happen. No commitment, no paperwork.

03

Meet your den

We'll connect you with your child's den leader. They'll share what to bring and what the next meetings look like.

04

Officially enroll

Online registration takes about ten minutes. Uniform arrives in the mail. You're in.

Find your den

Six dens.
One pack.

Dens are organized by grade and meet monthly at their own pace. Once a month, everyone comes together as a full pack.

Den · K

Lions

Kindergarten

The gentle first step. Short meetings, lots of exploration, parents welcome to stay.

Den · 1

Tigers

1st Grade

First real adventures — short hikes, simple camp skills, a first pack campout.

Den · 2

Wolves

2nd Grade

Building independence. Knots, first fires, more time outside with friends.

Den · 3

Bears

3rd Grade

The best year for getting dirty. Cooking, carving, fishing, hands-on everything.

Den · 4

Webelos

4th Grade

Starting to lead younger Scouts. Longer campouts, wider skills, what comes next.

Den · 5

Arrow of Light

5th Grade

The top Cub Scout rank. Capstone projects and the bridge to Scouts BSA.
